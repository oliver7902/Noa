WHAT IS
RUSH COIN BLOCKCHAIN ?

“Virtual Currency World ” Ecosystem for Game Service with On and Offline Affiliation
Implementation of a new ecosystem through A.I. Idle Action RPG game DApps

• Users pay for digital content service and game character development, and are rewarded with shopping at affiliated on and offline stores.
• The platform creates an online mall in which various on and offline stores are affiliated to provide advanced e-commerce , digital contents, and game services.
• Masters create a blockchain-based transparent transaction and consumer trend and minimize investment risk with profitable contents services.

TECHNOLOGY

"White Kingdom" Block Chain Service Flow

General users can use the services on the Entertainment Zone after registering their account and wallets.
Players who operate stores and own assets on the White Kingdom platform are "Masters" who generate profit.
RUSH Coin Smart Contract manages token issuance, payment/ settlement, rewards, and etc.

RSCN TOKEN

Token Distribution

  • Token Sale

    50%

    50% of the total issuance will be on sale. The unsold token during the investment period will be burned.

  • Advisors & Partners

    5%

    5% of the total issuance is allocated for the partners and advisors of our project. They will be rewarded based on their level of contribution.

  • Team

    5%

    5% of the total issuance is allocated to the team members. They will be rewarded based on their level of dedication and achievement.The rewarded token is locked from the date of ICO.

  • Marketing

    10%

    10% of the total issuance is allocated for marketing efforts such as internal/external loyalty program and bounty program for ICO promotion. Lockup period will vary for each marketing campaign.

  • Company

    30%

    30% of the total issuance is allocated for RSCN Platform mainnet development.
